WestWind Aerials
WestWind Aerials is an aerial photography company based in Sarpsborg Norway. We shoot with professional drones of various sizes to suite your job requirements. Whether you require high-res still photos or dynamic video with difficult pans - we can help.
WestWind Aerials is approved by the Luftfartstilsynet for RPAS operations in Norway. WestWind Aerials is also a member of UAS Norway, a non-profit organization for the Norwegian RPA (Remote Piloted Aircraft) community and has flight insurance through Berkley Insurance Norway.
